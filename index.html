About me

A developer with an IT backbone

I'm a Senior IT Analyst who fell in love with the front end. My day job means I understand infrastructure, performance, and security — and that perspective shows up in how I build websites.

Through the Code Institute Full Stack Web Development Diploma, I've turned that background into a genuine web development skillset. Every site I build is hand-coded in plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, just clean, purposeful code.

Outside of screens: you'll find me walking the Lake District, reading Warhammer 40K novels, or throwing darts.
